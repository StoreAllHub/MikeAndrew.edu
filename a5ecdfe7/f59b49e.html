Justin “Matts” Matthews is good at a lot of things: Rubik’s Cubes, playing guitar, herding cattle, and most importantly for his career in the NHL, hockey. Being an out trans woman in the music industry is more than enough pressure—a romantic entanglement would be added stress she doesn’t need.
